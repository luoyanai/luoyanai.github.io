Empowering Robots with Embodied Intelligence

Experience

Robotic Embodiment Learning

2022 - Present

Led research on intelligent manipulation and grasping using a semi-humanoid robotic platform. Designed a language-vision-motion integration pipeline, enabling teleoperation-driven data acquisition and reinforcement learning-based policy training for embodied control tasks.

Served as the technical lead for two Jiangsu Provincial Programs, focusing on dynamic balance control algorithms for humanoid robots. Developed and validated real-time control strategies for terrain-adaptive locomotion and coordinated whole-body stability in dynamic environments.

Provincial R&D Projects

2021 - 2022

3D Vision & Servo Control

2020 - 2021

Participated in a project funded by the Science and Technology Development Program of Jilin Province. Focused on robot operation systems based on machine vision, developing algorithms for disordered object recognition, 3D pose estimation, and real-time visual servoing.

Contributed to national-level competitions including the Internet+ Innovation and Entrepreneurship Challenge and the Mathematical Modeling Competition. Responsibilities included quadruped robot control algorithm design, physical simulation, and optimization of dynamic models.
